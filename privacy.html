Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 4, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

Mindful Breath Timer is designed with privacy in mind. We believe in protecting your personal information and being transparent about our practices.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identifying information
  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Location data
  • Breathing session recordings or biometric data
  • Usage patterns or behavioral tracking
  • Third-party tracking cookies

Local Data Storage

The app stores your preferences locally on your device using browser localStorage:

  • Audio settings (volume levels, sound preferences)
  • Preferred breathing patterns
  • Theme and display preferences

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by clearing your browser data.

Third-Party Services

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app works entirely in your browser without external connections except for:

  • Initial download from our servers
  • Service worker updates for offline functionality

Open Source

This application is open source. You can review our code and verify our privacy claims at our GitHub repository.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our website.
